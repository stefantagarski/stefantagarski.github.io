Primary School

Nikola Vapcarov Primary School in Strumica provided a nurturing environment where I began my educational journey. The passionate teachers, engaging lessons, and diverse extracurricular activities fostered a love for learning, while field trips and a strong sense of community created lasting memories and friendships.

High School

During my time at Jane Sandanski High School in Strumica, I had an unforgettable experience. From engaging classes to lifelong friendships, the school's vibrant atmosphere fostered growth and learning, leaving a lasting impact on my journey through education.

University

As a first-year student at FCSE-Skopje, my initial impressions of the faculty have been nothing short of outstanding. The state-of-the-art facilities, knowledgeable professors, and vibrant academic atmosphere have left me filled with excitement for the challenges that lie ahead in the coming years of my studies. I am eagerly looking forward to diving into the curriculum, engaging in cutting-edge research, and embracing the opportunities for personal and professional growth that await me at FCSE-Skopje.
